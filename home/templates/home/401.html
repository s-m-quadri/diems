401 Unauthorized

There may be one or more of the following reasons for this to happen

  1. You have wrongly typed the URL, or clicked on invalid URL, or did modification with it

  3. You have logged in, but before moving further, for security reasons, logged out

Troubleshoot Details

Solution: following are the generic steps: (not necessarily applicable to all problems)

  1. Check your internet connection. Try restarting PC, Tablet, mobile, internet adapter etc.
  2. Check the URL, is it correct one?
  3. Refresh the page by pressing CTRL + R or refreshing after removing all temporary files like cache and cookies.
  4. If still not resolve, contact the support team.
