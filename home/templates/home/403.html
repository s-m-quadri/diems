403 Forbidden - You don't have privileges for it

There may be one or more of the following reasons for this to happen

  1. You are a Teacher, but trying to attempt Superuser's privileges
  2. You are a Student, but trying to attempt Teacher's or Superuser's privileges
  3. You are a Guest, but trying to attempt Staff's privileges

Return URL: if feels comfortable, can attempt to login or register (for specified privileges) Located on the navigation bar.


Troubleshoot Details

Solution: following are the generic steps: (not necessarily applicable to all problems)

  1. Logout now, and login with account of suitable privileges.
  2. Ask admin/support team or staff of institute for right privileges
  3. Check your internet connection. Try restarting PC, Tablet, mobile, internet adapter etc.
  4. Check the URL, is it correct one?
  5. Refresh the page by pressing CTRL + R or refreshing after removing all temporary files like cache and cookies.
  6. If still not resolve, contact the support team.
