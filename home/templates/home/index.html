Marathwada Shikshan Prasarak Mandal (MSPM) is a non-profit educational organization located in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, India. It was established in 1958 with the aim of providing quality education to students in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra. MSPM manages various educational institutions from kindergarten to postgraduate level in various fields such as engineering, pharmacy, education, management, law, agriculture, and arts.

MSPM has a strong commitment to promoting education and is dedicated to providing quality education to students from all sections of society, especially to those who come from economically weaker backgrounds. The organization has established many schools and colleges in the region, providing opportunities for students to pursue their academic and professional goals.

MSPM is known for its excellence in academics and has consistently produced students who have excelled in various fields. The organization provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and a competent faculty to its students. MSPM also encourages research and development activities among its faculty and students.

Apart from providing quality education, MSPM also actively engages in social and community development activities. The organization runs various programs and initiatives to promote social awareness and community service among its students. MSPM has also taken various initiatives for the development of the Marathwada region, including providing medical assistance, relief work during natural calamities, and promoting rural development.

In summary, Marathwada Shikshan Prasarak Mandal is a leading educational organization in Maharashtra, committed to providing quality education to students from all sections of society. It is also dedicated to social and community development initiatives, making a positive impact on the region and society as a whole.
